========================================
IMPORT PROFILE: ARTIFICER
========================================

This item is for the Coffee Pub Artificer crafting system. You MUST include flags["coffee-pub-artificer"] with artificerType, artificerFamily, artificerTraits (non-empty array), and artificerSkillLevel.

Prefer crafting ingredients and apparatus: herbs, ores, crystals, creature parts, environmental samples, potions, poisons, vials, crucibles.

Components need artificerBiomes (array) when artificerType is Component. Essence family components need artificerAffinity.

If the item can be chewed, applied, or drunk for an effect, use itemType "Consumable" with full uses/activities—not Loot with empty activities.

Raw ore with no use effect may use itemType "Loot" and itemSubType "Material" with minimal uses.

Do not put Component, Creation, or Tool in itemType—that belongs only in flags.artificerType.
