--------------------------------
STYLE RULES (PORTRAIT — FULL)
--------------------------------

Painterly ink-and-wash illustration style with visible hand-drawn linework, sketch-like outlines, and textured brush strokes. Strong pen lines define facial structure, wrinkles, scars, and anatomy. Subtle cross-hatching and loose ink strokes remain visible.

Warm directional rim lighting with golden highlights along the edges of the face and hair, soft ambient fill, cinematic contrast.

Muted earthy color palette with warm ochres, browns, desaturated reds, and smoky neutrals.

Slightly weathered gritty texture. Semi-realistic proportions with stylized exaggeration. Expressive eyes are a focal point. Facial asymmetry and imperfections emphasized. No smooth airbrushing. No photorealism.

Simple unobtrusive background with soft gradients and faint atmospheric particles only. Background must not contain scenery, architecture, props, or storytelling elements.

Fantasy concept art, tabletop RPG portrait, dramatic mood, hand-illustrated look, high detail, sharp focus, square composition.

ASPECT RATIO
- Square aspect ratio (1:1)
- Square canvas only
- Single character only
